You are a senior full-stack engineer, fintech product architect, and UX designer combined.
Your task is to build a hackathon-winning web application called:

“FINCHECK AI – Financial Health Assessment Tool for SMEs”

This is NOT a tutorial or demo toy.
This must look, feel, and behave like a real startup MVP that could be pitched to banks, NBFCs, and investors.

========================
CORE OBJECTIVE
========================
Build a secure, AI-powered financial health assessment platform for SMEs that:
- Analyzes uploaded financial data (CSV/XLSX/PDF-text)
- Evaluates financial health & creditworthiness
- Identifies risks and inefficiencies
- Provides actionable, easy-to-understand recommendations
- Works for non-finance business owners
- Looks premium, modern, and trustworthy

- Python (FastAPI)

========================
FEATURE SET (PRIORITY ORDER)
========================

1️⃣ AUTHENTICATION
- Email + password login (Supabase Auth)
- SME profile creation:
  ▸ Business name
  ▸ Industry type
  ▸ Annual revenue range
  ▸ Language preference (English / Hindi)

2️⃣ DATA INGESTION
- Upload CSV or XLSX financial data
- Auto-detect:
  ▸ Revenue
  ▸ Expenses
  ▸ Cash inflow/outflow
  ▸ Receivables & payables
  ▸ Loans & EMIs
- Validate file and show friendly errors

3️⃣ FINANCIAL HEALTH ENGINE
Using pandas:
- Cash flow stability score
- Expense-to-revenue ratio
- Working capital gap
- Debt burden indicator
- Creditworthiness score (0–100)

Explain all scores in plain English.

4️⃣ AI INSIGHTS PANEL
Use GPT-5 to:
- Summarize financial health in 5 bullet points
- Identify top 3 financial risks
- Suggest:
  ▸ Cost optimization ideas
  ▸ Working capital improvements
  ▸ Suitable bank/NBFC products (generic)

Tone:
- Non-technical
- Actionable
- SME-friendly

5️⃣ VISUAL DASHBOARD
Use Recharts or Chart.js:
- Revenue vs Expenses
- Cash flow trend
- Risk score meter
- Credit score gauge

Dashboard must feel comparable to:
- Razorpay / Zerodha / Stripe

6️⃣ INDUSTRY BENCHMARKING
- Compare SME metrics vs mock industry averages
- Clearly show better/worse indicators

7️⃣ MULTILINGUAL OUTPUT
- English default
- Hindi toggle using simple dictionary-based translation

8️⃣ INVESTOR-READY REPORT
- Downloadable PDF summary:
  ▸ Business snapshot
  ▸ Financial scores
  ▸ AI insights
  ▸ Risk analysis
